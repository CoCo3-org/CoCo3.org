My name is James Ross and I am (for the time being) the only author / maintainer of the site. If you would like to help contribute that would be cool. Please contact me through the "contact" page (TODO).

This is yet another Retrocomputing site whose highlight system is the TRS 80 Color Computer. But the plan is to include other retro systems such as the NES / MSX to start as well as emulation, Raspberry Pi, FPGA, etc. I also want to cover systems from the 70's that predate the CoCo like the SWTPC 6800/6809 / Altair 8800 / IMSAI 8080 / Heathkit ET-3400 / KIM-1 / TRS80 Model 1 / Apple II / CP/M.

The site focus will be on technical information, both Retro and modern, as related to hobby projects.

All my projects will be open-source. I will briefly mention closed source projects and put them in the links page, but I will not be writing articles and covering them in detail. Unless someone wants to contribute such articles I'll gladly publish them on this site.

A news feed (twitter like) will include links and news of retro stuff and other tech items that interest me and might interest other retro enthusiasts.
