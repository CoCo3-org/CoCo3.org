About

My name is James Ross and I am the only author / maintainer of this site. However, If you would like to help contribute that would be cool. Please contact me through the "contact" page (TO-DO).

This is, roll eyes, yet another site for the TRS 80 Color Computer.

The site focus will be on technical information, both retro and modern, as related to CoCo hobby projects.

The landing page will be a news feed of sorts that will include links of recent and old news related to CoCo stuff.

NO Ads -- EVER! Also, NO tracking whatsoever. No hit counters, no Google Analytics, no cross-domain JavaScript...
