6/17/2024 1:00 AM: -- Breadboard CoCo - Part 2: Main Chips

The CoCo1 is a simple design. But only if you have a deep understanding of the chips involved.

There are 4 main chips at the core of CoCo1 that implement the basic functionality:

  • MC6809E: Central Processing Unit (CPU).
  • MC6883 / SN74LS783: Synchronous Address Multiplexer (SAM)
  • MC6847: Video Display Generator (VDG)
  • 2 x MC6821: Peripheral Interface Adapter (PIA)
